Dear world,

We have entered a challenging time.

As many of us shift more of our lives to the home over the coming weeks and months, significant challenges are emerging.

These challenges are impacting us, our family, friends, colleagues and those in our communities.

It's time to come together (digitally speaking) to solve these new and important problems. This means all entrepreneurs, innovators, engineers, designers, researchers, analysts, everyone!

We do this in an organised way. With a specified timeframe, and fully online, we focus on the emerging local problems of specific cities. But you can participate from anywhere!

Individuals and teams are free to form around problems they care about most. Then design, build, launch and share their websites, apps, web services, analyses and other digital solutions!

The time to act is now. What are you waiting for?

Join us on Slack Subscribe to updates

 — Blair and the #SHH community

How it works

#StayHomeHack is a fully remote hackathon helping our society stay connected, productive and healthy.

Join our community

Introduce yourself, and jump straight into problems. Discuss emerging challenges that are important to you.

Work together

Form a team around an ongoing discussion and ask the community for people with specific skills and interests.

Change the world

Work with your team to implement a solution and get it to your users. Ask the community for what help you need.

Meet our community

Hear from the SHH community about why they joined!
